# Code of conduct

## Our commitment

Contributors and maintainers commit to a respectful, accessible, and harassment-free project environment regardless of identity, background, experience, or role.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, specific, and patient.
- Critique ideas and code without attacking people.
- Respect privacy and never request or publish real applicant information.
- Use synthetic reproductions and redact secrets.
- Accept correction, acknowledge impact, and help repair mistakes.
- Keep project spaces focused on improving the software and its documentation.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, deliberate disclosure of private information, sustained disruption, and retaliation are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through a private channel exposed by the repository host. Do not include sensitive third-party information beyond what is necessary to review the report.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, limit participation, or ban participants when behavior threatens the safety or effectiveness of the community. Reports should be handled confidentially, consistently, and without retaliation.

This policy applies to repository discussions, issues, reviews, project chat, and public representation of the project.
